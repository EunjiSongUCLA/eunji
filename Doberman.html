Doberman Pinscher

The Doberman Pinscher was developed in Germany during the late 19th century, primarily as a guard dog.
With his sleek coat, athletic build, and characteristic cropped ears and docked tail, the Doberman Pinscher looks like an aristocrat.
He is a highly energetic and intelligent dog, suited for police and military work, canine sports, and as a family guardian and companion.
